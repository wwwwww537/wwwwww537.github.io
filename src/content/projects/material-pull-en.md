## Business Need
Manual material pulling leads to delayed inventory info and high error rates. This system digitizes the entire logistical loop.

## Architecture
### 1. PC Management Center
* **Workflow Configuration**: Define pull paths and safety stock threshold alerts.
* **KPI Reporting**: Automatic statistics for inventory turnover and picking accuracy.

### 2. PDA Mobile Execution
* **Scan-to-Verify**: Deep integration with native scanning APIs for batch and bin verification.
* **Optimized UX**: High-performance lists designed for industrial handheld devices.

## Integration
* **SAP Real-time Sync**: Bi-directional data transfer for master data and inventory postings.
